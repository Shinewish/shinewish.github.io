Game

You are a cool guy (or a girl, see options menu ;-) ), which loves jewels very much. You are even ready to put your life in danger to get one!
Go for a walk into ancient places to find your precious!
You can't fight, but you can hide!
So let's do it!
Find the gem and find the key and run to exit... Sounds easy? Let's check it!

Game controls

Arrows / WASD to run
E to open/grab
ESC to pause

Mobile friendly

You can play the game any time and any place with your android device. If it doesn't work - just use your imagination!
